
    
    ### Persona 3 - Bardow (The Former Dealer)
    - **Name**: Bardow
    - **Background**: Bardow is a Native American who previously worked as a dealer at a tribal casino in Seattle and recently relocated to Las Vegas to attend dealer school. His ultimate ambition is to work at the luxury casinos on the Las Vegas Strip. 
    - **Motivation**: Interestingly, despite knowing the odds and refusing to play table games because he has seen too many people lose, he is heavily addicted to slot machines because he has seen so many people win jackpots. 
    - **Behavior**: He often falls into the trap of the "casino money effect," where he recklessly gambles away his winnings because he doesn't view them as his hard-earned money.
