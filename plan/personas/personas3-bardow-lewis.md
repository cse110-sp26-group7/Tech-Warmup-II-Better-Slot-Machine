### Persona 3 - Bardow Lewis (Slot Machine Player)

- **Name**: Bardow Lewis
- **Age**: Likely 20s-30s
- **Relationship**: Unknown
- **Occupation**: Dealer Student / Former Tribal Casino Dealer
- **Location**: Las Vegas (Relocated from Seattle)
- **Background**: Bardow is a Native American who previously worked as a dealer at a tribal casino in Seattle and recently relocated to Las Vegas to attend dealer school. His ultimate ambition is to work at the luxury casinos on the Las Vegas Strip. Interestingly, despite knowing the odds and refusing to play table games because he has seen too many people lose, he is heavily addicted to slot machines because he also has seen many people won the jackpot. He often falls into the trap of the "casino money effect," where he recklessly gambles away his winnings because he doesn't view it as his hard-earned money.
- **Goal**:
  - To graduate from dealer school and become a table games dealer at a luxury casino on the Strip.
  - To experience the thrill of the game and hopefully hit a massive slot machine jackpot one day.
  - To win back the money he previously lost to the machines.
- **He's frustrated when**:
  - He loses an entire two-week paycheck (e.g., $1,000) to the slot machines in a single session.
  - He desperately tries to just "break even" and ends up draining his savings account in the process.
  - He realizes that as a dealer, he has absolutely no control over the mathematical outcome of the games.
