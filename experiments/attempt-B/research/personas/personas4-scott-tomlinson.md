### Persona 4 - Scott Tomlinson (Advantage/Recreational Player)

- **Name**: Scott Tomlinson
- **Age**: Middle-aged
- **Relationship**: Unknown
- **Occupation**: Casino Poker Room Supervisor
- **Location**: Las Vegas
- **Background**: Scott is a highly rational casino poker room supervisor at a luxury Las Vegas casino who has been playing casino games almost every day for the past 22 to 23 years. Unlike problem gamblers, he plays strictly for fun with low stakes (usually bringing only $60 to $100) and treats it as a game of strategy rather than a way to make money. In his free time, he spends hours watching gambling videos on YouTube while cooking or walking his dog, using them to study the math, payout frequencies, and strategies of various slot machines.
- **Goal**:
  - To enjoy the entertainment, fun, and strategic aspects of gambling without risking his financial stability.
  - To continuously figure out the math and payout regularity behind different slot machines by analyzing YouTube videos.
  - To make his small budget stretch for hours of gameplay without losing it all.
- **He's frustrated when**:
  - Uneducated tourists show up at the casino with a false sense of entitlement and lose their money because they have no plan.
  - Players fall victim to cognitive distortions, believing their past losses mean a machine is "due" to pay them back.
  - Gamblers fail to educate themselves on the mathematical reality of the house edge.
