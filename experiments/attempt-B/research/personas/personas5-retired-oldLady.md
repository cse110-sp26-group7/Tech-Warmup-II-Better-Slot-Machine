### Persona 5 - Retired Old Lady (Senior Promotional Player)

- **Name**: Molly White
- **Age**: In her 80s
- **Relationship**: Married
- **Occupation**: Retired
- **Location**: Las Vagas suburban
- **Background**: This retired woman in her 80s has been visiting Las Vegas with her husband every year for the past 26 years. After living a highly frugal life and working hard for decades to build her savings, she feels she no longer needs to save for the future and uses the casino as her primary form of twilight entertainment. She meticulously tracks casino promotions, such as "50 Plus Club" days, BOGO meals, and free play, allowing the casino to serve as a sort of luxurious, albeit calculated, companion in her old age.
- **Goal**:
  - To find an engaging, socially active way to spend her retirement years with her husband.
  - To strictly adhere to her daily gambling budget of around $100.
  - To maximize the value of senior citizen casino promotions, comps, and free perks.
- **She's frustrated when**:
  - She and her husband hit a losing streak and feel they have put too much money into a single machine.
  - The emotional sting of losing tempts them to go over their strict $100 daily limit.
  - The games fail to provide the extended entertainment value she expects for her budget.
